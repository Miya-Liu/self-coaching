# Learnings (Experience)

**Experience** — optimisation and training insight the agent should reuse. See `SKILL.md`.

**Purpose**: Optimisation-related insights from running and tuning training (hyperparameters, batching, metrics, what improved or regressed, process lessons).

**Not for**: raw training stderr/stdout; put those in `logs/` and summarize here.

**When to append**: when experiments stall, metric flatlines, you change training strategy, or you discover a reproducible training-side lesson.

**Categories**: optimization | process | metric | stability | best_practice
